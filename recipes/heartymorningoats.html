Hearty Morning Oats!

Looking for a Breakfast that will energise you for hours? This hearty Oats recipe packed with fibre and healthy fats will ensure that you're able to tackle the most difficult tasks of the day. Start your day right, and enjoy this warming bowl on a cold morning to kickstart your metabolism!

Ingredients

Oats

  • 70g Oats
  • 250ml Milk
  • Pinch of Salt

Garnish Ideas Optional

  • Banana
  • Berries
  • Peanut Butter
  • Chia Seeds
  • Honey
  • Nuts
  • Cacao Nibs
  • Dark Chocolate
  • Seasonal Fruits

Method

  1. Put Oats into a saucepan.
  2. Add a pinch of salt and the milk to the pan and set it to a medium heat.
  3. Stir often and let cook for 5 minutes or until desired consistency.
  4. Garnish with desired toppings!
