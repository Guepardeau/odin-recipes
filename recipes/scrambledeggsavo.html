Scrambled Eggs with Avocado Toast

A couple of perfect scrambled Eggs turn Avocado Toast into a complete, satisfying meal. Enjoy this at Breakfast, Lunch or whenever you feel like it! It's that good!

Ingredients

Eggs

  • 2-3 Eggs
  • 2-3 tbsp Milk
  • ½ tbsp Butter
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Some Toast to serve

Smashed Avocado

  • 1 Avocado
  • ½ Red Onion
  • 1-2 tbsp Lime Juice
  • Salt

Method

Smashed Avocado

  1. Cut the Avocado in half, lengthways and stick a knife into the Avocado seed. Twist and take out.
  2. Spoon the Avocado flesh into a bowl.
  3. Dice the Red Onion and add to the bowl with Avocado. Add Lime Juice and Salt, to taste.
  4. Mash and combine well until all ingredients are thoroughly mixed.

Scrambled Eggs

  1. Crack Eggs into a Bowl.
  2. Add Milk, Salt and Pepper and use a fork to beat the Eggs until homogenised.
  3. Set to a medium-low heat and add the Butter and Eggs.
  4. Stir often (to avoid large curds in the Egg) until done.
  5. Toast desired amount of Bread and serve with Smashed Avocado.
