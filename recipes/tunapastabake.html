Tuna Pasta Bake

Whip up this quick and easy school-night dinner using pantry ingredients, tinned tuna and sweetcorn. A real treat with the kids, and comfort food at its finest. This Tuna Pasta Bake will leave you feeling a million bucks.

Ingredients

  • 500g Pasta of Choice, Penne/Rigatoni work well
  • 50g Butter
  • 50g Plain Flour
  • 600ml Milk
  • 250g Strong Cheddar, grated
  • 2x 160g Tuna Steak in spring water, drained
  • Large handful of chopped Parsley

Method

  1. Pre-heat oven to 170 Degrees Celsius.
  2. Boil 500g Pasta for 2 mins less time than stated on the pack.
  3. To make the sauce, melt 50g butter in a saucepan and stir in 50g plain flour.
  4. Cook for 1 min, then gradually stir in 600ml milk to make a thick white sauce.
  5. Remove from the heat and stir in all but a handful of the 250g grated cheddar.
  6. Drain the pasta, mix with the white sauce, two 160g drained cans tuna, one 330g drained can sweetcorn and a large handful of chopped parsley, then season and serve.
