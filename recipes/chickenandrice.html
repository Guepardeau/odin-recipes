Blackened Chicken and Uncle Ben's Rice

Blackened Chicken and Uncle Ben's Rice is one of my favourite post-workout dinners. Succulent, and filled with protein needed to recover your muscles, you'll never get bored of such a delicious and tasty meal. How could you? It's delicious.

Ingredients

Blackened Chicken

  • 1 Chicken Breast, halved
  • 2-3 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Spices (Cajun, Paprika, Garlic Powder, Cayenne Pepper.)
  • Salt & Pepper

Uncle Ben's Rice

  • 1 Uncle Ben's Rice Packet (Any flavour, I prefer 'Savoury Chicken')
  • 2 tbsp Water

Method

Blackened Chicken

  1. Season Chicken well with Salt and Spices and leave to dry brine for at least 45 minutes.
  2. Pre-heat frying pan, add the Oil, lay the Breast away from you and leave to cook on a medium-high heat for 3 minutes.
  3. Flip Chicken over and allow to cook for 3 minutes on the other side.
  4. Allow to rest on a wire rack, so as to not let the Chicken Breast get soggy.

Uncle Ben's Rice

  1. Break up the Rice in the packet with your hands.
  2. Open the Packet and empty Rice in to a pan. Set the heat to medium.
  3. Add 2 tbsp of boiling water and cover the pan with a lid.
  4. Let Rice simmer until water mostly evaporates and then stir thoroughly.
